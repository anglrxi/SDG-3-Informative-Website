Examples of Progress

1. Reduction in Child Mortality

Progress: Global under-5 mortality dropped from 93 deaths per 1,000 live births in 1990 to 38 per 1,000 in 2019. Example Programs:

-UNICEF and WHO vaccination programs against measles, polio, and pneumonia.
-Promotion of breastfeeding and improved nutrition programs in low-income countries.

2. Maternal Health Improvements

Progress: Global maternal mortality ratio declined by 38% between 2000 and 2017.

Example Programs:

-Training more midwives and birth attendants in Africa and Asia.
-Better access to prenatal and postnatal care through government and NGO programs.

3. Combating HIV/AIDS

Progress: HIV infections fell by about 23% globally between 2010 and 2019; AIDS-related deaths declined by 39%. Example Programs:

-The Global Fund to Fight AIDS, TB, and Malaria provides treatment, prevention, and education.
-Free antiretroviral therapy (ART) programs in many developing countries.

4. Fighting Malaria and Tuberculosis

Progress: Malaria deaths fell by about 60% since 2000, and TB mortality is steadily decreasing. Example Programs:

-Distribution of insecticide-treated mosquito nets.
-Early detection and treatment programs for TB.
-Investment in vaccines (like RTS,S for malaria).

5. Universal Health Coverage (UHC)

Progress: Many countries have expanded healthcare coverage:
-Thailand and South Korea achieved near-universal health insurance.
-Low-income countries increasingly provide free or subsidized essential medicines.
Impact: More people can access doctors, vaccinations, and medications without financial hardship.

6. Reducing Tobacco Use and Preventing NCDs

Progress: Global smoking rates declined from 27% in 2000 to 20% in 2020.

Example Programs:

-WHO Framework Convention on Tobacco Control (FCTC).
-Campaigns promoting healthy lifestyles to prevent heart disease, diabetes, and obesity.

7. Global Pandemic Preparedness

Progress: After SARS, Ebola, and COVID-19, countries improved early warning systems and vaccine development.

Example Programs:

-COVAX initiative for equitable COVID-19 vaccine distribution.
-Strengthened disease surveillance and rapid response networks globally.

8. Road Safety Improvements

Progress: Some countries halved road traffic fatalities through law enforcement, road safety campaigns, and better vehicle standards.

Example Programs:

-WHO’s “Save LIVES” road safety initiative.
-Introduction of speed limits, seatbelt laws, and helmet regulations.

9. Mental Health Awareness

Progress: More countries are recognizing mental health as part of primary healthcare.

Example Programs:

-School and workplace mental health programs.
-National policies supporting counseling, therapy, and crisis intervention.
